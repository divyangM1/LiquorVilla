Whiskey is an alcoholic beverage made by distilling fermented grains, mainly barley and corn. It is typically aged in wooden barrels, which gives it its signature flavor, complexity and color. Depending on the type, whiskey can taste sweet or smoky, and can vary from mild to strong. Enjoyed neat, or in a cocktail, whiskey is known for its smoothness and its ability to pair with food. Popular whiskey styles include Scotch, Irish whiskey, Bourbon, Rye and Blended whiskey.
